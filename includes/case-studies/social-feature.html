Payments experience

Case Study

Designed a payment status dashboard component that made billing visibility and renewal actions clear so members could manage payments and maintain continuous coverage.

Overview

Florida Blue members needed a clearer way to manage payments and renew coverage through the digital member portal. The existing experience created friction around billing visibility and payment completion, making it difficult for members to confidently manage their accounts. Improving the payments experience required simplifying key interaction flows so members could quickly understand their billing status, complete transactions, and maintain continuous coverage.

  • Designed a dashboard component highlighting current payment status and billing actions
  • Improved visual hierarchy for billing and payment actions
  • Strengthened UI consistency across payment states

Context & Challenge

The payments experience plays a critical role in member retention and satisfaction. However, existing payment workflows made it challenging for users to locate billing information quickly and complete transactions efficiently. Members often needed clearer visual feedback around payment status, upcoming balances, and renewal actions, which created opportunities to refine the interface and reduce friction throughout the payment journey.

Design considerations

  • Improve clarity around billing status and payment actions
  • Simplify navigation to critical payment tasks
  • Ensure consistent UI patterns across payment states
Result

Achieving the inclusion of 4 context cues and 3 actions in a small surface area helped members understand their options and make payments with greater confidence.

Reflection

Designing the payments experience reinforced how critical clarity and consistency are when users manage important tasks like insurance coverage and billing. When user interfaces clearly communicate status, next steps, and confirmation states, members are able to complete transactions with greater confidence and trust in the platform.

“Thoughtful interface design can turn complex account tasks into clear, confident user experiences.”
